Buffalo: The Queen City

Buffalo is a vibrant city located in the western region of New York State. With a population of over 250,000, it is the second-largest city in the state. Buffalo was officially incorporated as a city in 1832 and has a rich history.

It's considered an urban center and is known for its cultural attractions, historic architecture, and proximity to the Great Lakes. The city's average income level is in line with the rest of the state.
