About New York State

New York is a diverse state in the northeastern United States. It has a rich history, vibrant cities, and beautiful natural landscapes. With a population of over 19 million, it's one of the most populous states in the country.

From the urban hustle of New York City to the serene beauty of the Adirondack Mountains, New York offers something for everyone.

Quick Facts

  • Population: 19 million+
  • Capital: Albany
  • Region: Northeastern U.S.
  • Notable Cities: New York City, Buffalo, Rochester
